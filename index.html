About Me

I’m a Ph.D. student of Natural Lanugage Processing Group at Utrecht University, advised by Prof. Albert Gatt and Dr. Denis Paperno . I work on VLM, Multimodal Learning ad Interpretability.

I’m passionate about understanding and reasoning about dynamical systems through the lens of causality—particularly those governed by ODEs and SDEs. Lately, I’ve been fascinated by causal representation learning and diffusion-based generative models.

I am actively seeking research visiting and/or internship opportunities in 2025/2026, feel free to reach out if you think I am a suitable fit!
